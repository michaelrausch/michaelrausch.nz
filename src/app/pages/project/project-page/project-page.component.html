Party Parrots At Sea

Named after the infamous slack emoji, Party Parrots at Sea is a fun, interactive 3D sailing simulator. The game was built by a team of eight students as a part of the SENG302 course. SENG302 is a full year project course designed to give students experience working on a large team-based project. Originally, the project was to build a visualizer for the 35th Americas Cup. However, as the Americas Cup came to an end, we were instructed to turn our visualizer into a fully-fledged sailing game.
